About Us

EDU-IT has experience as a host organization within the Erasmus + program - for over 20 years it has been a host and intermediary organization: conducting trainings and internships for foreign students, vocational schools and teachers. We have extensive experience in organizing apprenticeships and internships for young people and adults from Poland and abroad.

For over 10 years, EDU -IT has been organizing internships for over 1500 students of vocational schools from all over Europe - Lithuania, Hungary, Belgium, Spain, Portugal, Italy, Sweden, Finland, Norway, Turkey, as well as vocational training and internships for around 100 vocational teachers.

Our mission is to strive for excellence in the field of educational services provided to Polish and European clients. We aim at providing high-quality content, comfort of training, the use of modern and effective solutions and customer service that guarantees customer satisfaction.

We are guided by values such as:
  • quality, responsibility and modernity
  • unique and warm atmosphere for our clients
  • creativity, friendliness and openess
  • we derive satisfaction from our work and contact with other people
  • we understand the needs of the market in the field of lifelong learning

In 2020, EDU-IT received Erasmus accreditation for the Consortium coordinator in the field of vocational education and training. The aim of the Consortium is the cooperation of the parties and the joint and solid implementation of Erasmus + mobility projects in the financial perspective for the years 2021-2027 under the granted accreditation.

Erasmus accreditations are a tool for education and training organizations wishing to open up to cross-border exchange and cooperation. The award of Erasmus accreditation confirms that the organization has set up a plan to implement high-quality mobility activities and use them to improve the education and training offer.
